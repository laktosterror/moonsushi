Welcome

Welcome to Moon Sushi, where the flavors of Japan come alive in the heart of Longyearbyen! We invite you to embark on a culinary adventure that celebrates the art of sushi-making. As you enter our restaurant, you’ll be greeted by a cozy ambiance and the enticing aroma of our expertly crafted dishes.


Our menu is designed to cater to all palates, featuring a wide range of sushi options, from traditional favorites to innovative specialties. Whether you’re here for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, our friendly staff is committed to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience.


Join us at Moon Sushi and discover why we are a beloved culinary gem in Svalbard. We can’t wait to serve you!
